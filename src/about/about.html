01. About

We are bringing skin in the game back to the Attention Economy.

03. Investors

Why didn’t we pursue ICO?

Indeed, the token sale option was on the table, but we’ve realised that we still needed funds to build proper proof-of-concepts and fully explore the idea before running a token sale.

Fortunately, we’ve met investors who understand the space well and were excited about the problem to back us financially on our journey. We’re grateful to have them on board.
